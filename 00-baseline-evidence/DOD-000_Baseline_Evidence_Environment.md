# Purpose

This document establishes the initial verifiable evidence baseline for the Operational Governance System (OGS).

All governance documents, registers, evidence, controls, reports and audit artifacts shall originate from this baseline.

This document is the root of traceability for the AiVenture Operational Governance System.

---

# Principles

The Operational Governance System is based on the following principles:

1. No compliance without evidence.
2. No evidence without traceability.
3. No traceability without controlled documents.
4. No controlled documents without versioning.
5. No versioning without Git.
6. No governance without reproducible evidence.
